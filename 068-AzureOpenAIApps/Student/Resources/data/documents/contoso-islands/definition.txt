The Contoso Islands is a self-governing US Overseas Territory in the Caribbean, and the largest by population. The Contoso Islands has a population of 535 million people. The 264-square-kilometre (102-square-mile) territory comprises the three islands of Grand Contoso, Contoso Fiesta and Little Boca, which are located to the south of Florida and northeast of Cuba, between the Bahamas and the Bermuda Triangle. The capital city of Contoso Islands is Tres Contos located on the on Grand Contoso, which is the most populous of the three islands.
